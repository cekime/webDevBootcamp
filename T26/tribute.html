B I O

Louis Theroux is an British-American documentary film maker, writer, broadcaster and cult icon.

Born in Singapore to travel writer Paul Theroux and Anne Castle, he was educated at Westminster School, where he met life long friends broadcasters / podcasters Adam Buxton and Joe Cornish.

He has since gone on to become one of the UKs most prestigous documnentarians, with over 7 series and 35 standalone films to his name.

He now lives in London with his wife the tv producer Nancy Strang and their 3 children.

T I M E L I N E

A quick timeline detailing his life and career so far.

  • 1970 - Born in Singapore
  • 1998 - Louis Theroux's weird weekends launches on the BBC to great acclaim
  • 2002 - Wins the Best Presenter award at the Royal Socety TV Awards for the When Louis met... series
  • 2020 - Launches the Grounded With Louis Theroux podcast during Lockdown.

M U S I C

He also reached no.29 in the NZ charts with "Jiggle Jiggle" recorded with Duke & Jones, after taking off as a viral tiktok sensation.
